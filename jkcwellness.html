Branding & Web Design

Therapeutic Relaxation

JKC Wellness

I recently completed a comprehensive rebrand for JKC Wellness, my private studio specializing in massage, cupping, waxing, and energy work. From the new logo and color palette to typography and website design, every element was reimagined to create a cohesive identity. The goal was to build a brand that balances professional excellence with a warm, inviting atmosphere that authentically reflects my approach to holistic care.

Client: JKC Wellness

Project Type: Web Design, Brand Identity, Print Design, Mobile App

Competencies: Photography, Typography, Layout, Illustrations

Software: Illustrator, Photoshop, CapCut, Figma

Clarity before creativity

brand strategy


Positioning Statement

JKC Wellness is a boutique wellness and holistic sanctuary offering a harmonious blend of massage therapy, facial and body cupping, ear seeds, waxing, and energetic healing. Unlike conventional spas, JKC Wellness stands out by continually evolving—constantly learning new techniques, expanding its expertise, and crafting innovative treatments—delivered in a serene, intimate environment rooted in heartfelt care. Guided by expert hands, each client receives thoughtful attention, targeted wellness solutions, and an ambiance of genuine care— making every visit a uniquely transformative experience.

Target Audience

JKC Wellness Hawaii's core clients are health-conscious individuals, ages 30 to 60, who deeply value self-care and holistic well-being. These men and women recognize self-care as an essential practice—not an indulgence—and are willing to invest in personalized, authentic, and expertly delivered wellness experiences.

Branding Personality

Transparent: Open about techniques, honest about benefits and limitations, shares behind-the-scenes knowledge. Builds emotional trust through clarity and authenticity.

Trustworthy: Delivers on promises with consistency, grounded in expertise and professional growth. Clients feel secure and confident in every treatment.

Kind: Warmth, empathy, and nurturing energy. Reflects through gentle language, attentive care, and thoughtfulness in every interaction.

Core colors that define the visual identity

Primary Colors


JKC Wellness' main colors has always been a deep blue with hints of white and light gray. I wanted to have some pop of colors—although not a part of my traditional wheel house, there are subtle hints of gold & copper in my treatment room.

character and clarity

typeface


For the typeface, I wanted a sense of elegance and classical proportion. Orpheus Pro delivered a refined yet understated sophistication, creating contrast that reinforces visual hierarchy and interest. For the primary typeface, I sought a design that was clean, versatile, and welcoming—qualities that perfectly align with my brand's personality, which Lato successfully fulfilled.

Supporting tones that add flexibility and depth

Accent palette


In an unconventional approach, I wanted a broader range of colors than the typical 3-6-color palette. I preferred additional color options—not bright primary hues, but more nuanced tones that would complement the warm feel of my room. Along with expanding the selection, I created muted shades, which, to my surprise, worked beautifully as subtle accents alongside the simple line illustrations and icons.
